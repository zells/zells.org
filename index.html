What is zells?

Zells is an idea that I've been carrying around with me for a while. It's basically the solution to everything that annoys me about computing. It's either a distributed databases, an operating system or a better web. I'm not even sure.

My first serious approach to formulating the idea culminated in my Diploma Thesis in 2011. Since then there have been various shots at it but I always dashed against the complexity of the solution. So my new approach is to first thoroughly analyse the problem. Hence this website.
